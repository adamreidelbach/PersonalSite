Adam Reidelbach has performed in Europe, Japan, Mexico and all across the United States. As a drummer, he has shared the stage with a variety of national and international touring artists including David Cook, Kimberly Dunn, Tim Halperin, Abi Ann, Kevin Ahart, Rich Harney, Harlan Hodges, Pete Rodriguez, Ephraim Owens, Emily Wolfe and more. He has worked with a variety of bands including the Cody Bryan Band, Grizzly Adams Family, the Vintage 15 and more. Classical and new music performances include appearances with the Omaha Symphony, Lincoln Symphony, Opera Omaha, ANALOG Arts Ensemble, among others.

Adam is very active as an educator and before relocating to Nashville, TN in 2015 he founded and directed the In The Shed Jazz Camp based out of Austin, TX. He was a percussion tech / private lesson teacher at the award winning James Bowie High School, a private lesson teacher at Cedar Park High School / Middle School, and a maintained a private lesson studio out of his home. Before moving to Austin, TX in 2010, he was the director of percussion at Bellevue West High School in Bellevue, NE, percussion instructor at Omaha Central High School, and the music caption head and arranger for the WGI Open Class percussion group, The 402, from Papillion, NE. His early experiences were as a snare drummer with the Colts Drum and Bugle Corps during the summers of 2004 - 2006 where he held the position of drum captain during his final two years and was awarded Percussion Member of the Year in 2005.
